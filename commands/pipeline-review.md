description: Review the Close pipeline for risk, stale deals, and recommended next actions.
---

Review the Close pipeline for `$ARGUMENTS`. If no filter is provided, review active opportunities visible to the current user.

Use Close MCP tools to identify:

- opportunities with overdue or missing next steps;
- stale deals with no meaningful recent activity;
- close dates, confidence, or values that deserve attention;
- the most important actions to take next.

Summarize the criteria used, keep facts separate from recommendations, and include Close links when available. Do not modify CRM data.
