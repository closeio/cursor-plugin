---
name: account-brief
description: Build a concise Close account brief from recent activity, contacts, opportunities, and tasks.
---

Create a concise account brief for `$ARGUMENTS` using Close MCP tools.

Find and disambiguate the lead first. Then cover:

- company and key contacts;
- active opportunities and their stage, value, owner, confidence, and close date;
- recent calls, meetings, and email threads;
- open or overdue tasks;
- risks, open questions, and a recommended next step.

Use record facts from Close, clearly label any inference, and include Close links when available. Do not modify CRM data.
